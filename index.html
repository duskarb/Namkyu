Hello, I’m Namkyu Yeo,

I value the act of discovering unfamiliar possibilities within the familiar,
and transforming those possibilities into new experiences.
For me, design is not just a tool for problem-solving,
but a language and medium that connects people and the world.
This is a space of experimentation that constantly raises questions and reshapes our senses.

My journey is less about finding definitive answers,
and more about expanding perspectives and exploring
In doing so, I seek to reimagine the relationships between people, technology, space, and time.
